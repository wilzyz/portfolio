About Me

Driven by a deep passion for web development, I am a self-taught developer with a proven track record of delivering high-quality websites. I have successfully completed freelance projects for small businesses, honing my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, while utilizing MySQL for backend development. One of my key projects involved building a dynamic e-commerce platform for a small business, where I focused on delivering an intuitive user experience and seamless functionality. My commitment to continuous learning and passion for coding allows me to approach every project with creativity and precision.

I've always been fascinated by technology and how it shapes the world. Since I was a child, I dreamed of being a developer, but I didn't know what kind of developer. As I grew older, I decided to become a web developer because it allows me to create websites from the comfort of my home. It also lets me be more creative, both in designing and building interactions on the front-end and back-end.

Every project given to me fuels my passion for exploring new methods and programming languages to grow further in web development. For me, every challenge in web development helps me grow and improve my skills.
